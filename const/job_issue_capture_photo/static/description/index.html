Scan & Capture Photo for Task and Issue on Portal Website

Scan Photo / Image and Send to Backend Construction Project Team

Job Contracting Projects | Construction Projects with Scan / Upload Photos in Portal Website

This app allows your customer / portal user / agent / subcontractor to take photos from mobile for tasks and send them to backend project team and also it allows your customer to take photos for construction related issues and send them to backend team.


Main Features:

  • Allow to capture photos for Job Orders / Tasks and Issues.
      • □ Tasks Menu on Portal:
    • Your customer / portal user / agent / subcontractor who has access to the tasks menu on the portal of your website (Tasks menu is an odoo standard out of box feature on the portal) can take photos and scan from mobile and send them to your backend project team.
      □ Issues Menu on Portal:
    • Your customers who have access to the issues menu on the portal of your website can take photos and scan from mobile and send them to your backend project team.
  • Allow them to view all Photos/Images in My Account Portal of your website too.
  • For more details about the dependent app(s) please click on the dependent apps link. [You can find it under the Included Dependencies section].
  • Important note: This app is not an Android or Iphone app, but it's only Odoo design on the Web Application of Odoo itself.
