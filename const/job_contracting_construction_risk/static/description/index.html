Project Risk Management for Construction and Job Contracting Projects

Construction and Job Contracting Project Risk Manage Odoo App

This app allows your project team users and project managers to have features to manage risk on projects and project tasks including creation of risk Incidents from project and tasks for Construction and Contracting projects.


Main Features:

  • Allow your project users to create Risks from the Risk menu under configuration.
  • Allow your managers to view Risk analysis of projects and tasks for reporting purposes.
  • Allow you to create and manage to set Risk type, risk category and risk response setup under configuration.
  • Risk incidents creation and management during project and task execution.
